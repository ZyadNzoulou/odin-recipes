Chicken Breast

The final result (yummy !)

The chicken breast is a classic recipe for bodybuilders, college students, and single people, which is why today we will learn about how to easily prepare it.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (duh)
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Slice your chicken in half (your chicken should look like a butterfly
  2. Season your chicken (on both sides) using paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Turn on your stove to high heat
  4. Place butter in your pan
  5. When it's hot place your chicken breast and let it fry for a 1-2 minutes, repeat the process for the other side.
  6. When both sides get that brown look, reduce your stove to medium heat and add a small amount of water. Let it cook for a good 5-7 minutes. (If all the water evaporates, add some water back).
  7. When it's all done, place in a plate and let it cool for 2-3 minutes and then enjoy!
